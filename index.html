    -THE THREE MAIN GREEK GODS:


    1.Zeus:


    Zeus is the god of the skies and weather.He controls the weather depending in his mood.He is often found in Mount Olympus unlike his sister Hestia,who barely goes to Mount Olympus.He is the son of The Titan Cronus and Rhea.He is commonly seen with a thunderbolt.


    2.Hades:


    Hades is one of Zeus's brothers.He is the god of the Underworld,he only has one wife and her name is Persephone.Hades enjoys his time in the Underworld more than the living life.He is commonly seen with a brident.


    3.Poseidon:


    Poseidon is also Zeus's brother.He is the god of the seas and other water.He has a wife named Amphitrite.Some say,if you want to sail you need to sacrifice something (mainly animals) before you sail because if you dont sacrifice something to Poseidon there's a 50% chance you will die-_-.He is commenly seen with a trident.
